About the Author

The Linear Algebra Suite was created by Chris A. LaFollette to provide clear, visual, and engineering-grade tools for understanding, and visualizing linear algebra inside AutoCAD.

The modules are designed to be precise, modular, and intuitive for both students and professionals.
